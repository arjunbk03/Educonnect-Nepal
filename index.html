Collaborative Schools Network

Email: network.collaborativeschools@gmail.com

Website: collaborativeschools.info

About:The Collaborative Schools Network (CSN) revolutionizes public education in Nepal, transforming struggling schools in Kathmandu and offering expansive curriculum support nationwide. Partnering with local authorities, CSN efficiently manages daily tasks, bolstered by significant financial contributions. With a focus on elevating teaching quality, fortifying school management, nurturing community engagement, and enhancing infrastructure, they've achieved stellar outcomes: a staggering 415% increase in student enrollment, steadfast 94% attendance, and notable academic advancements. A testament to their success, a CSN student secured prestigious scholarships for academic pursuits in both Germany and the US.

Moin Uddin

Email: moin.writer@gmail.com

Website: Moin Uddin Facebook Page

About:Moin Uddin began as a Teach For Nepal Fellow in 2018, launching projects like Career Exposure Trips and Teacher Exposure Trips. He has since then gained international attention with daily vlogs, resulting in a school library's foundation. After his fellowship, he taught in remote Nepali regions and became a teacher trainer in Rural Dhading. In 2022, he won the 360 Impact Award and was also named Young Leader of the Year for his dedication to rural education.

Maya Universe Academy

Email: mayauniverseacademynepal@gmail.com

Website: mayauniverseacademy.org

About:Maya Universe Academy, founded in 2011 by visionary youths, is a transformative educational institution in Nepal offering affordable quality education in rural regions. Defying traditional financial barriers, the academy operates on an innovative barter system where parents exchange skills and knowledge—like farming and construction—for their children's schooling. With two active locations, including a primary school up to grade 8, Maya not only educates but also shelters 80 students, many affected by the 2015 earthquake. Proudly inclusive of all ethnicities, Maya alumni continue their educational journey nationwide, while some even return as educators, perpetuating the cycle of empowerment. The institution currently supports 320 students and employs 33 dedicated staff members across its locations.

Kids at School in Nepal

Email: info@kasin.org.uk

Website: kasin.org

About:Established in 2005, Kids at School in Nepal (KASIN) stands as a beacon of hope for countless Nepali children. Despite Nepal's enchanting landscapes and heartwarming populace, it grapples with severe poverty and developmental hurdles. Addressing the critical needs of those in remote villages, KASIN is committed to elevating educational standards. They collaborate directly with schools, bolstering infrastructure, facilitating teacher training, and supplying essential learning resources, ensuring every penny is put to its best use.

United World Schools

Email: iinfo@unitedworldschools.org

Website: uwsglobal.net

About:United World Schools (UWS) believes fervently that education is the bedrock of a prosperous future. Despite 258 million children being denied schooling globally, UWS stands committed to reaching the most remote and marginalized communities. Collaborating with local partners, they not only construct schools but also nurture comprehensive educational ecosystems by training educators and fortifying foundational educational frameworks. Their sustainable model emphasizes empowerment, aiming for long-term growth and change, rather than transient aid. With a vision to eradicate educational poverty, UWS is not just preparing children for the present, but revolutionizing their tomorrows. Join their transformative journey.

National Campaign for Education Nepal

Email: info@ncenepal.org.np

Website: ncenepal.org

About:The National Campaign for Education Nepal (NCE Nepal), established in 2003, is a vast coalition of 433 member organizations dedicated to strengthening Nepal's public education system. Born as the Nepal chapter of the Global Campaign for Education, NCE Nepal champions the educational rights enshrined in the constitution for all citizens. Through rigorous research, advocacy, and networking, it strives for an inclusive, equitable, and quality education landscape in Nepal, acting as both a guardian and catalyst for change.
