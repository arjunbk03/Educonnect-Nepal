EduConnect Nepal aims to connect organisations striving to enhance the primary and secondary education in Nepal and individuals who want to contribute to meaningful causes.

We beleive that every child deserves quality education.

Organisation

The following organisations here are working to make the government and public Education in Nepal better. Get to know them on the map and visit their website. Below the map, you can also find a list of organisation with their email, address, and logo.

Collaborative Schools Network

Email: network.collaborativeschools@gmail.com

Website: collaborativeschools.info

About: We manage public schools in Nepal to transform the quality of education they provide to some of the country's poorest children.

Teach For Nepal

Email: info@teachfornepal.org

Website: teachfornepal.org

About: Teach for Nepal is a movement of outstanding university graduates and young professionals who are committed to end education inequity in Nepal starting with two years of fellowship teaching in public schools. Teach For Nepal fellowship is a two year long, full-time paid employment opportunity.

Maya Universe Academy

Email: mayauniverseacademynepal@gmail.com

Website: mayauniverseacademy.org

About:Maya Universe Academy was established in 2011 by youths determined to challenge the education system in Nepal. The chain of private schools provides access to affordable and quality education to children living in rural Nepal.

Save the Children Nepal

Email: post.nepal@savethechildren.org

Website: savethechildren.net

About:Save the Children has been operating in Nepal since 1976. Our programs to improve the lives of children in Nepal are guided by the National Plan of Action for Children and the UN Millennium Development Goals. As the largest child-focused organization in the Nepal, we cover a wide geographical area with multiple programs that support our vision for all children to attain the right to survival, protection, development and participation.
