EduConnect Nepal aims to connect organizations striving to enhance primary and secondary education in Nepal with individuals who want to contribute to that cause. This website will provide you with an overview of Nepal's current education condition, the problems we face, and the solutions. Additionally, you will find information about the organisations that are tirelessly working to improve educational sector. EduConnect Nepal encourages everyone to support these organisations because they are truly making a difference in children's lives in Nepal, and your small support would mean a lot to them.

Collaborative Schools Network

Email: network.collaborativeschools@gmail.com

Website: collaborativeschools.info

About:CSN manages public schools in Nepal to transform the quality of education they provide to some of the country's poorest children.

Moin Uddin

Email: moin.writer@gmail.com

Website: Moin Uddin Facebook Page

About:Moin Uddin began as a Teach For Nepal Fellow in 2018, launching projects like Career Exposure Trips and Teacher Exposure Trips. He gained international attention with daily vlogs, resulting in a school library's foundation. After his fellowship, he taught in remote Nepali regions and became a teacher trainer in Rural Dhading. In 2022, he won the 360 Impact Award and was also named Young Leader of the Year for his dedication to rural education.

Maya Universe Academy

Email: mayauniverseacademynepal@gmail.com

Website: mayauniverseacademy.org

About:Maya Universe Academy was established in 2011 by youths determined to challenge the education system in Nepal. The chain of private schools provides access to affordable and quality education to children living in rural Nepal.

Kids at School in Nepal

Email: info@kasin.org.uk

Website: kasin.org

About:Kids at School in Nepal (KASIN) was founded in 2005 as a small charity and is making a big difference to the lives of hundreds of Nepali children. Nepal is one of the most beautiful countries on earth and its people are utterly delightful but it is the 12th poorest country in the world. 85% of the population live in remote rural villages, 60% have no electricity, 38% have no toilet and 45% are illiterate.

United World Schools

Email: iinfo@unitedworldschools.org

Website: uwsglobal.net

About:UWS works to ensure children have access to quality, inclusive education in remote and marginalised communities by collaborating with local partners, communities and governments.

National Campaign for Education Nepal

Email: info@ncenepal.org.np

Website: ncenepal.org

About:NCE Nepal with ECOSOC status organization is a national network of different 437 member organizations that includes I/NGOs, teacher organization, journalists and community organization working for strengthening of the public education system in Nepal.
